ABOUT ME

My name is Archie. I am a 3D Artist based in Guildford, England. I am an Alumni of the University of the Arts London. I specialise in 3D Hard Surface Art and Character Art.

The main software suite I utilise at the moment includes: Blender, Autodesk Maya, Substance 3D Painter, Photoshop, and Zbrush.

My background is in web design/development hence the fun animations across my portfolio site!
