Surrey Dog Training Class Options

Before we begin any training, all dogs are assessed for compatibility and suitability for our course programs or their training needs.

Dogs and their handlers who have successfully achieved a pass in their Exemplary Obedience Exams in both individual and group tasks are invited back for their Graduation Ceremony in April and August. There we acknowledge and celebrate their success, as well as present them a certificate.

If your dog is unwell or in season please stay home and take care of them, we will ensure we do catch up sessions at no extra cost to ensure you don't miss out.
