About Max Fewtrell

Max Fewtrell is a former British racing driver, content creator, and managing partner of Quadrant - Lando Norris' content and lifestyle company.

Starting his racing career in karting in 2009, Max went on to become the 2016 British F4 Champion and 2018 Formula Renault Eurocup Champion. After retiring from professional racing in 2020, he transitioned to content creation and streaming.

As a Twitch streamer with over 150,000 followers, Max plays competitive FPS games like Escape from Tarkov and Call of Duty, while also competing in iRacing sim racing. He co-hosts the Quadcast podcast and is deeply involved in both gaming and motorsport communities.

His unique background gives him expert insight into what makes a great gaming and sim racing PC. He knows what it takes to run demanding titles at high frame rates, stream professional-quality content, and handle intensive sim racing platforms.

Together with Wired2Fire, we've created a range of systems that deliver the performance Max demands for gaming, streaming, and sim racing - hand-built by experts and backed by our industry-leading 5-year warranty.
