Your Complete PC Journey

From choosing components to receiving expert support

When choosing components for a gaming computer, there are a few key factors to consider

Processor

A powerful processor, such as an Intel Core i7 or i9, or an AMD Ryzen 7 or 9, is crucial for gaming. The processor handles all of the game's calculations and physics, so the better the processor, the smoother the gameplay.

Graphics Card

A dedicated graphics card is essential for gaming. The graphics card is responsible for rendering the game's visuals, and a high-performance card will ensure that the game looks great and runs smoothly. The higher-end card you buy the faster the game will run.

Memory

A minimum of 16GB of RAM is recommended for gaming, but 16GB or more is ideal. This will ensure that the computer can handle multiple tasks and applications without slowing down.

Storage

Solid-state drives (SSDs) are recommended for gaming as they are faster and more reliable than traditional hard drives. An SSD with at least 256GB of storage is recommended, but 1TB or more is ideal.

Power Supply

A high-quality power supply is essential to ensure that all of the components have enough power to run properly. A power supply with at least 600 watts is recommended for gaming.

Cooling

A good cooling system is important to prevent the components from overheating and to ensure that the computer runs smoothly during long gaming sessions.

What Happens After You Place Your Order?

Once you place your order, our experienced team thoroughly checks it to ensure compatibility, and components are allocated to your order. Once this is done, your order enters our production queue.

Building Your PC

When it's your PC's turn, one of our highly skilled engineers will build your machine, carefully managing cables for improved appearance, air flow, and reduced dust build up.

Installing the OS

The system is now ready to receive its operating system. We install Windows 11 via a sophisticated image-based deployment system with absolutely no junkware pre-installed.

Extensive Testing

After the build, your machine undergoes extensive testing. We install the latest drivers and run exhaustive stability testing on all components including the CPU, GPU, memory, and storage.

Careful Packaging

When ready for dispatch, the system is packaged into high-quality rigid cardboard. We also use sophisticated internal packaging for PCs with heavier discrete GPUs.
