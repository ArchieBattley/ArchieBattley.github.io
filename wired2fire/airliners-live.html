About Airliners Live

We are Airliners Live, a live plane spotting channel where community comes first.

For over 7 years, we've streamed plane spotting from Manchester Airport and built a following of millions. Behind the cameras, we're not just broadcasters, we're tech geeks and avid flight simmers with over 15+ years of experience.

From pushing our production boundaries to running high-end flight sims, we know exactly what makes a great PC. That's why we've partnered with Wired2Fire.

We didn't just pick any company. These folks are genuine specialists who live and breathe performance. Their expert team builds every single system by hand, carefully tuning and stress-testing each machine making sure it's absolutely rock-solid. It means you get a world-class rig that's ready to go, straight out of the box.

We've put our passion together with their building expertise to create the selection of PCs that have received a seal of approval from the team at Airliners Live.
