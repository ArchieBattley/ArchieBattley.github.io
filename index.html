Welcome to The Paw-fect Dog School

Caring for your dog

We are a reputable Surrey based dog school offering a wide range of dog training in a friendly non-judgmental, fair, compassionate and respectful way.


My vision is to give every dog a chance, we train any breed, any age, any issue, all dogs are welcome.


We offer structured courses, designed to mentally stimulate and find your dogs hidden talents.
